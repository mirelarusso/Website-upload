The Lost Paradise Kuna Yala

Welcome to my first page, I am glad to have you as my guest.

I am not a good narrator, I never was but right this moment as I look out from my window and my eyes become grey from the fog I see around, it starts in my mind the desire to be part of the lost paradise, Kuna Yala.

Oh, how I love this place from the first time I saw the pictures shared by various persons. Did you ever feel to just run away and escape from the daily programs and let go all just for a day spent in paradise? No duties, just you and the sea and the sand and the Creator within you..?
So just take a look to the pictures and imagine "all the people living life in peace" as John Lenon once said. Then lay down and relaxxx! Stay focus to those wonderful places where serenity is everywhere.

This is how we all should live...and it is possible, it is just about what we chose.
I chose this!
